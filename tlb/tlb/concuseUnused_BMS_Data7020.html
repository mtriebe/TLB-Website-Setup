Use of Concept: Unused BMS Data

Creator

Description

This is data that was on the BMS, but not utilized, and therefore lost. The BMS is removed from the battery at either "Prepare/Discharge Battery for Recycling" (A3311) or "Disassemble Battery for recycling" (A3312).
