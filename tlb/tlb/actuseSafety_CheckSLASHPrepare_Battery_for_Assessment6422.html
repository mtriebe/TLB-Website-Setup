Activity-in-Diagram: Safety Check/Prepare Battery for Assessment

Description

Notes:
None

Definitions:
Discharge - controlled depletion of stored energy to a pre-defined amount in an electrochemical cell/battery. Sometimes described as a deep depth of discharge.

Depth of Discharge (DOD) - percentage of a rechargeable battery's capacity that has been used or cycled out of the battery during a discharge cycle. Adapted from Park et al., (2023) (see references below).

Standards:
SAE J1634_202104, Battery Electric Vehicle Energy Consumption and Range Test Procedure.
