Concept: Direct Feedstock

Creator

Description

Direct Feedstock includes all the feedstock necessary for recovery of batteries. Below are the feedstocks for Repurpose (A31) and Remanufacture (A32).

Repurpose (A31)
- New Battery Cells or Modules Systems
- Used Battery Cells or Modules Systems
- New Other Battery Components
- Reusable Other Battery Components

Remanufacture (A32)
- New Battery Cells or Modules Systems
- Used Battery Cells or Modules Systems
- New Other Battery Components
- Reusable Other Battery Components

  • Appears as Direct Feedstock in Diagram Recover Materials/Devices in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
  • Appears as Direct Feedstock in Diagram Repurpose in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
  • Appears as Direct Feedstock in Diagram Remanufacture in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
  • Appears as Direct Feedstock in Diagram Remanufacture Battery in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
