Use of Concept: Tested and Sorted Batteries

Description

This includes batteries that are good candidates for repurposing and remanufacturing, and lower-value batteries that are more suitable for recycling.
These tested and sorted batteries may included Damaged, Defective, or Recalled (DDR) batteries. Some DDR can be recycled while others cannot and are waste.
