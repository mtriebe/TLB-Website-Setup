Concept: Unused BMS Data

Creator

Description

This is data that was on the BMS, but not utilized, and therefore lost. The BMS is removed from the battery at either "Prepare/Discharge Battery for Recycling" (A3311) or "Disassemble Battery for recycling" (A3312).

Usage List:

  • Appears as Unused BMS Data in Diagram Pretreat Battery in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
  • Appears as Unused BMS Data in Diagram Recycle in in Treat Traction Lithium Batteries at End-of-Life.
