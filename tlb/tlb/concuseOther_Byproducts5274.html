Use of Concept: Other Byproducts

Creator

Description

This includes battery components that do not fall under Battery Materials or Recovered Batteries. These components may be recycled outside of this recovery process. This may include standard metals or plastics recycling. The following are included for Repurpose (A31) and Remanufacture (A32).

Repurpose (A31)
- Unusable Battery Components

Remanufacture (A32)
- Damaged Other Battery Components
