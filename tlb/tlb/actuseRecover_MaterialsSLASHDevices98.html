Activity-in-Diagram: Recover Materials/Devices

Description

Notes:
Following the testing and sorting process, feedstock is routed into one of three recovery streams based on their (battery) health and state: repurposing for secondary use, remanufacturing for restoration to original specs, or recycling for material recovery. Feedstock that fails to meet the minimum requirements for any of these three pathways are diverted to disposal (Waste).

Definitions:
See daughter activities.

Standards:

UL 1974, Evaluation for Repurposing or Remanufacturing Batteries
